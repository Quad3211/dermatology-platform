# Product Brief: SkinHealth (Dermatology Access for Jamaica)

## One-line summary
SkinHealth is a web platform that makes dermatology care more accessible in Jamaica by combining AI-assisted skin image triage with secure access to licensed dermatologists.

## Problem
People in Jamaica often face long waits and limited access to dermatologists. That creates uncertainty about whether a skin concern is urgent, leading to delayed care, unnecessary anxiety, or avoidable clinic visits.

## Solution
SkinHealth delivers fast, low-friction triage by letting patients upload a photo of a skin concern, receive a preliminary risk assessment, and connect with a licensed dermatologist when follow-up is warranted. It is designed to help users decide whether and how urgently to seek professional care, without replacing clinical diagnosis.

## Target users
- Patients in Jamaica who need quick guidance about skin concerns.
- Licensed dermatologists serving Jamaica who want a secure, efficient way to review cases and provide follow-up.
- Clinic or health administrators who need oversight across patients and clinicians.

## Key features
- AI-assisted image screening with triage levels (Low, Moderate, High, Critical).
- Plain-language screening report with confidence indicators.
- Secure account-based access to results and consultations.
- Doctor review workflow for flagged cases.
- In-app secure messaging between patient and doctor.
- Live video consultation support.
- Appointment booking with doctor confirmation.
- Patient dashboard to track consultations and notes.
- Doctor portal to review cases, update notes, and manage patients.

- Education content for common dermatology topics and prevention.
- Admin dashboard to view activity and manage users.

## User journey (high-level)
1. Patient creates a secure account (or tries the public scanner first).
2. Patient uploads a clear photo of the skin concern.
3. AI generates a triage level and explanation. natrual or over the counter treament options are recomended if applicable
4. If escalation is needed, the case is routed to a licensed dermatologist.
5. Patient books a consultation and connects via secure chat or video.
6. Doctor provides notes and follow-up guidance.

## Trust, safety, and compliance posture
- Clear medical disclaimers: AI screening is a triage aid, not a diagnosis.
- Privacy-forward workflow: results are gated behind authentication.
- Messaging emphasizes encryption and privacy protections.

## Why this helps Jamaica specifically
- Reduces the need for immediate in-person visits when capacity is limited.
- Gives people outside major cities faster access to specialist guidance.
- Provides a consistent path from initial concern to a real dermatologist.
- Helps clinicians triage demand and focus attention on higher-risk cases.

## Success metrics
- Time to initial triage result.
- Conversion rate from scan to consultation.
- Average wait time to doctor response.
- Patient satisfaction and follow-up completion.
- Clinician workload balance and case resolution times.

## Positioning statement
SkinHealth delivers accessible dermatology care for Jamaica by pairing AI-assisted triage with real dermatologists, enabling faster answers, safer decisions, and fewer unnecessary delays.
